Who We Are

EUROPACK Industrial Solutions S.A.

EUROPACK was founded in 1972. Its primary focus is representing international manufacturers of packaging and food processing machinery and equipment for other industries. With a specialized and well-developed technical support department, EUROPACK Industrial Solutions goes beyond standard servicing — we design complete production lines and installations and offer high-level consultancy services to our clients.
